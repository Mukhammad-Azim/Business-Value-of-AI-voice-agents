# ElevenLabs × Better.com Case Study — Summary
## Source: BusinessWire / StockTitan, February 2026
## Original: https://www.businesswire.com (via ElevenLabs press release)

---

## Partnership Overview

Better Home & Finance Holding Company (NASDAQ: BETR) uses ElevenLabs Agents as the voice interface for "Betsy™," its generative AI loan agent. Better.com utilizes a modular architecture combining:
- **Tinman®** — Better's proprietary loan origination engine (orchestration, compliance, underwriting)
- **ElevenLabs Agents** — voice interface layer (ASR, TTS, conversation management)

## Key Metrics (FY 2025)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total calls placed by Betsy in 2025 | **1.89 million** |
| Monthly call volume | ~100,000 mortgage-related phone calls |
| Borrower inquiries automated end-to-end | **35.5%** |
| Monthly hours saved for loan officers | **1,666 hours** |
| Reduction in average cost to originate | **41%** |
| Increase in lead-to-lock conversion (2025) | **100%** (as stated by ElevenLabs) |

## Notes on Data Interpretation

- The "100% increase in lead-to-lock conversion" figure stated by ElevenLabs is higher than the "33.3% improvement" (3.3% → 4.4%) cited by Garg in Q2 2025. This discrepancy may reflect: (a) different time period (full-year 2025 vs. Q2 2025), (b) different baseline used, or (c) different metric definition. The thesis uses the more conservative Garg figure.
- The "41% reduction in average cost to originate" aligns approximately with the thesis calculation showing a decline from ~$8,500 to ~$4,900 (42% reduction).

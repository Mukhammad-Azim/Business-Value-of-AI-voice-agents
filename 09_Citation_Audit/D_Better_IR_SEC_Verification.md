## D.3 Detailed Verification Notes

### Entry 5 — Betsy Launch Press Release
- BusinessWire identifies the issuer as Better Home & Finance Holding Company, dated October 17, 2024, 09:00 AM ET.
- The HousingWire (Nunes, 2024) coverage dated the same day independently corroborates the launch date.
- The Q3 2024 8-K (entry 6) narrates "Launched Betsy™…in October 2024" in management commentary, providing SEC-filed corroboration.
- Recommendation: cite using **Business Wire URL** because that is the original wire distribution. Optionally add a parenthetical "(see also Q3 2024 8-K, exhibit 99.1)".

### Entry 7 — Q4/FY 2024 Earnings Release
- Better IR URL confirmed: https://investors.better.com/news/news-details/2025/Better-Home--Finance-Holding-Company-Announces-2024-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-Results/default.aspx
- The same release was filed as Exhibit 99.1 to a Form 8-K on March 18, 2025 (SEC accession-number page also accessible via EDGAR full-text search).
- Per user URL-selection rule, the Better IR URL is used because this is an earnings press release (not a primary SEC filing).

### Entry 10 — Form 10-K for FY 2025
- The accession number `000162828026017747` corresponds to the FY 2025 10-K filing.
- The exact filename `aurcu-20251231.htm` reflects the issuer ticker stem and the period-ending date.
- Recommendation: at submission time, double-check the EDGAR filing index page at https://www.sec.gov/cgi-bin/browse-edgar?action=getcompany&CIK=0001835856&type=10-K to confirm the accession number remains current.

### Entry 11 — Better × ElevenLabs Joint Showcase
- Better IR URL confirmed: https://investors.better.com/news/news-details/2026/ElevenLabs-and-Better-com-Showcase-Success-of-AI-Loan-Agent-Betsy-at-Scale-in-Financial-Services/

- Caveat: This is the source of the "115,000 → 700,000 monthly customer interactions" and "+30% conversion uplift" claims. These metrics are **company-reported and not independently audited**; they should be cited with that caveat in §5.4.2 and §5.6 (see Deliverable G, item G.4).

# Better.com 2025 Earnings Transcripts Analysis: AI & Business Value Findings

This document acts as a repository for empirical evidence extracted directly from Better Home & Finance Holding Company's Q1 and Q2 2025 Earnings Call Transcripts. It is structured around the four primary business value indicators established in the thesis methodology.

## 1. Sales & Funnel Conversion 
*Direct evidence of the AI agent improving top-of-funnel conversion metrics and overall customer experience.*

*   **Lead-to-Lock Conversion:** In Q2 2025, CEO Vishal Garg explicitly quantified the conversion impact of Betsy AI: *"Since we launched Betsy AI, our lead-to-lock conversion rate has increased by over 30% from 3.3% to 4.4% which is massively meaningful to drive incremental volume and revenue and squeezing profitability out of each loan."*
*   **Customer Satisfaction:** AI adoption actually improved human-metrics. Garg reported in Q2: *"Since we've adopted Betsy, our Net Promoter Score has increased from 39 to 64, putting our score in line with companies like Google and Apple."*
*   **Intelligent Workarounds:** Transcripts highlight that humans struggle to perform complex math adjustments on the fly, but Betsy AI excels. Betsy can instantly suggest taking out a little cash to pay off two specific debts, lowering the debt-to-income (DTI) ratio just enough to qualify a buyer that a human loan officer would have simply rejected.

## 2. Operational Scalability & Automation Routing
*Evidence of the platform handling higher volumes of work without proportionally increasing human headcount.*

*   **Explosive Interaction Growth:** 
    *   *Q1 2025:* Betsy executed 127,000 consumer interactions in the month of March alone.
    *   *Q2 2025:* Betsy executed approximately 600,000 consumer interactions over the quarter.
*   **Underwriting Automation:** In Q1, AI underwriting managed roughly 40% of locked loans. By Q2, this scaled to 43%, with management identifying a clear pathway to operating at 75% automation in the near future.
*   **Processing Speed:** The AI agent processes multi-variable underwriting constraints instantaneously. According to management, the AI resolves complex borrower matching issues in *"3 to 5 seconds"*—a task that would traditionally take human underwriters *"3 to 10 hours to resolve."*
*   **Decoupling Volume and Headcount:** The most direct proof of AI scalability was noted in Q2 when the CEO stated: *"we grew unit volume 35% in the last quarter, and we subtracted people on the sales team."* Previously, in the mortgage industry, volume growth required equal 1:1 hiring growth.

## 3. Aggregate Cost Efficiency
*Measurements of structural reductions in the cost to manufacture loans and overall operational expenditures.*

*   **Crushing the Industry Baseline:** Better.com's cost to originate dropped to approximately half of the industry average by Q2. 
    *   *Mid-term target:* Management projects driving this down to approximately 1/3 of the industry average within the next 12 months.
*   **Total Cost Goal ("North Star"):** Management aims to drive total loan production costs down to just $1,500 ($500 sales, $500 ops, $500 external vendors/credit). This target would make Better.com roughly 6x cheaper than the standard legacy retail originator, which costs around $7,500 per loan to manufacture.
*   **Non-Compensation Expense Reduction:** Better.com successfully reduced non-comp manufacturing costs to sub-$1,000 per loan through AI optimization and vendor deprecation. 
*   **B2B / White-label Cost Reduction:** For local retail loan officers leveraging the "Tinman AI Platform" (such as NEO loan officers), Better estimates the platform compressed *"a staggering 80% of their back office costs."*

## 4. Strategic Market Value & B2B Expansion
*Evidence of AI transforming the company's overall market positioning and B2B revenue potential.*

*   **Increasing Loan Officer Productivity:** Historically, the industry median for a traditional loan officer is around 3 loans a month. Better's goal with their AI-assisted network (NEO) is to triple capacity, empowering individual loan officers to close 10 loans a month.
*   **Technological Moat:** Traditional loan origination software (LOS) like Encompass cannot easily integrate generative AI because they consist of deeply fragmented, siloed systems. As Garg noted: *"OpenAI will tell you the maximum number of function calls that the LLM can do at the same time is 2 to 3... how are you going to do it across 8 systems without minutes of latency?"* Better’s unified API structure acts as a monumental strategic moat, letting them license it externally.
*   **Bank Partnerships:** Better signed their first agreement to run software for a mid-tier bank ($1Bn mortgage volume). Unlike typical legacy software that takes $1M-$5M and 9 months to install, Better achieved integration within 3 to 60 days at zero implementation cost. 
*   **Path to Profitability:** As a direct result of these margin improvements and efficiency levers created by the AI platform, Better publicly guided in their Q2 call that they hold the visibility and expectation to achieve Adjusted EBITDA Breakeven by Q3 2026.
